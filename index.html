About Me

Howdy, my name is Camila and I am new to the wonderful world of coding! I have a Bachelors of Science in psychology and even began a Masters program to become a counselor. However, I realized early on that my passion for finishing projects and perfecting functions did not translate very well to a career in counseling.

I began my journey with Codecademy thinking nothing more than some fun or a new hobby might come of it. I ended up really enjoying it to a point where I have redirected my career goals and hope to become a software engineer in the near future!

As a child of immigrant parents, I grew up with very high expectations which has bled into my personality and turned me into a bit of a perfectionist. I believe this level of determination and, frankly, scrutiny, will allow me to develop these skills rather quickly. By the time any potential employers read this, my website should be pretty cool looking! Stay tuned.
